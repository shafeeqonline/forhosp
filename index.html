About us

Our story

We imagine a world where seeking health care is not restricted to your current location, where you will never have to compromise on your budget while making health care decisions.
At forHosp we are all bounded by our single core purpose, to provide you with the healthcare you truly deserve - even if that means you travel abroad.
We are looking to alleviate the common insecurities people have while travelling for healthcare by providing them with

  • High quality medical treatment.
  • Providing clarity.
  • Helping them with accessibility.

What makes us different and better?

  • We are an Indian based startup with a dream. We want to see medical tourism to function on an authentic and value driven premise across the world and we are confident of gaining your belief in our vision.
  • The advantage of focusing of more on individual clients and providing a meaningful service.
  • India is placed among the top 3 medical tourism destinations in Asia. So we decided to set stage in the hub.
  • People are less likely to face a communication barrier in India.
  • Ease of Visa.
  • Alternate medicine : India is home to a number of alternate medicine techniques such as ayurveda, yoga, unani and homeopathy which are popular among foriegners.
